Let me introduce myself!

My name is Samantha Whelpley, and I am a current student at the University of Pittsburgh studying Computer Science. I grew up in a small town east of Cleveland. I wanted to share some facts about myself!

I have played the flute for 8 years and been in marching band for 6 years! Coming to Pitt, I was super excited to be able to become a part of the University of Pittsburgh Marching Band. I have now marched in Pitt Band for two semesters and traveled with the band to Penn State University, University of Notre Dame, Charlotte, North Carolina, and El Paso, Texas.
